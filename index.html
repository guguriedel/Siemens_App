Oil and Gas to Offshore Wind

Optimize offshore platforms decomissioning with a smart tool that generates a ranked list of the best repurposing options balancing sustainability, efficiency and economic viability.

The growing need for decarbonization and the high costs of offshore platform decommissioning make it essential to adopt a strategic approach for repurposing these structures. Our application offers an innovative solution to support decision-makers in the oil and gas industry.

Through an interactive, multi-criteria-based tool, our system enables stakeholders to assess sustainable repurposing alternatives. Users can define evaluation criteria, assign weights to each, and obtain a ranking of the best options, such as wind turbines, solar panels, or macroalgae cultivation.

The platform not only simplifies decision-making but also ensures that economic, environmental, and regulatory factors are considered. In this way, we help transform decommissioning challenges into opportunities for the energy transition.

How It Works

Step 1: User Input

The user select the most influence criteria for the decision making process such as cost-effectviness , local economic impact ...

Step 2: Weight Assignment

Each criterion is assigned a weight based on its relative importance to the user's strategic goals.

Step 3: Data Analysis

The system processes the inputs and cross-references them with technical and economic fesability data. Thus generating a ranked list os viable repurposing options.

Step 4: Results Visualization

The tool presents a comparative ranking of potential projects, such as offshore wind farms, artifical reefs or marine biomass cultivation.
